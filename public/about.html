About Me

I am betting on myself with my severance by trying to learn to code and hopefully get employed in the web development field at some point.

I enjoy running, biking and playing with computers. I have a high school deploma from Roseburg High School as well as some credits from Umpqua Community College and the online school University of Pheonix.
